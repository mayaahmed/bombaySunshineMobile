Chapter 33

Messing around

As time went by the hostel mess food tasted awful. The mess had a weekly menu. So every Monday stuffed brinjal was served with lunch. The taste of the food never changed from one week to the other. Clearly mother's love was a big factor of creativity in the kitchen. A new girl at Tara's table was complaining loudly.

"This food is so spicy. They should put less chillies in it. My mouth is burning."

"What's with her?" asked Tara to Mala.

"That female is US returned" said Mala. "Her name is Vimla. She now has problems with spicy food."

Tara watched the new comer. Tara now was no longer a freshie. She was a senior and she could now look down on freshies and their behavior.

"You see this food is reasonably mild for us" said Meena. "In fact it is too mild for me. That is why I have to eat pickles with it."

"This has to stop" said Vimla. "One cannot eat this kind of food without getting sick. I think I will become the mess secretary and then I will change all this. We will then have some decent food. I must take up that responsibility and make the mess food edible."

"We will look forward to your becoming the mess secretary with great fervor" said Mala.

"It is not a post that is very popular and you will have no difficulty becoming the mess secretary" said Tara smiling. "In fact this is the first time I have heard anyone wanting to become one. Also improving the quality of the mess food is a topic that is close to the hearts of a lot of people in the hostel."

"I am forced to become a mess secretary because this food is inedible" said Vimla.

The group of girls at the table sympathized with the US returned freshie because after all she was planning to change all this when her turn came.

"Unfortunately we will have to wait till the next elections for that" said Meena with a sigh.

But then Meena had not expected the turn of events that happened next. Suddenly, a couple of days later there were notices all around the hostel saying that an outrage had been committed by Dina who was a mess secretary. She was to apologize or resign. This issue would be discussed in a GBM (General body meeting). The notice also said that some grave issues will be discussed along with the apology issue.

"I wonder what is going on. I know Dina. She cannot commit any outrage" said Tara.

"We will find out this evening at the GBM" said Mala.

Many girls of the hostel gathered in the dining hall for the GBM. Rajini who was the general secretary of the hostel began with the grave issues the hostel face.

"We have gathered here today to discuss some grave issues. We will get to Dina's apology issue soon. Before that I must warn you that things are changing and we will be slapped with rules. But we must stand united and face any challenge that face this hostel. We will not accept any rules that are not acceptable to us ..."

"I wonder what are these challenges facing our hostel" whispered Mala.

"Let me ask her" said Tara and she asked aloud

"What are these threats you are talking about?"

"We will come to that in a second. We must stand united because unity is strength..." continued Rajini.

"But who will slap rules on us? The students decide all the rules here. What are these threats you are talking about?" a girl asked.

"I can only tell you that we will face tough times soon. We must be united..." continued Rajini.

At this point Tara decided to give up listening and read parts of a book through Rajini's monologue which was getting nowhere. There were some girls in the audience who were getting restless because they wanted to take up the apology issue as soon as possible. After may be Rajini's voice gave out, or perhaps she sensed the boredom of the crowd, Rajini threw the floor open to discuss the misconduct of the mess secretary.

"Dina had guests for dinner on Tuesday night" said a girl called Arti. "She had a special table set with special vessels for her guests. This has never happened before. This is abuse of office power. She must apologize."

"These new vessels were bought recently with the extra money the mess made" explained Dina. "I went and personally bought this for the hostel. The very purpose of these new vessels are to entertain guests of the hostelites."

"But you had so much extra food on your table, Milan had to go hungry because the mess ran out of food" said Arti.

"She could have very well come to my table and taken whatever food she wanted" said Dina. "I did not know that the mess ran out of food. I do not believe it did either."

"Did it run out of food really?" asked Tara.

"No, not all food but the cabbage sabji" said Milan a little embarrassed about being painted the starving victim.

"See there is a difference in going hungry and not having a sabji for dinner. But she could have come and taken the food from my table anytime" said Dina.

But Tara soon sensed that the issue was not important here. It was some sort of personal fight and a small group of girls wanted Dina to apologize or resign.

"I will not apologize. Come what may. I will not apologize" said Dina firmly.

"Let us vote on this. Should Dina apologize else resign?" asked Rajini.

And to Tara's surprise the verdict was either Dina apologizes or she resigns.

"I will not apologize. I will send in my resignation" said Dina and she left the hall in a huff.

"If she resigns we need another mess secretary. She was doing a good job. It will be hard to find another" said Rajini.

"That is not the issue here. This is a matter of principle" said Arti and a group of girls around her nodded.

Later Tara visited Dina and said "Why don't you apologize? What does it matter? It is just a simple word which means nothing if you do not mean it. We would hate to lose you as a mess secretary."

"I am not going to say sorry" said Dina.

"Okay we will apologize as a mess team then" said Durga who was part of the mess team.

"You can apologize if you like" said Dina smiling.

So Tara helped Durga pen an apology which went like this

"We are sorry,
you missed your curry,
though it was cabbage,
that tasted like garbage,
we are sorry,
that you missed your curry."

They signed it the "Mess team" and put up the apology on the notice board. Of course Tara and Durga laughed a lot trying out variations of the apology and finally decided on this one. When Tara met Rajini she asked

"Okay, so that was an apology. Does Dina stay?"

"The group was not satisfied. We will have another GBM" said Rajini.

Tara wisely decided not to attend the GBM but she heard from Rajini that Dina had to resign.

"So does Vimla now become a mess secretary?" asked Tara. "Every one knows that Vimla wanted to become one."

"No. She said she was busy and will become a mess sec next year" said Rajini.

"Then who is the mess sec now?" asked Tara.

"We will have to do with only one this term. No one else took up the responsibility" said Rajini and Tara just shook her head in reply.

Tara wrote down in her diary that night:

"Somehow women change with age. In the Undergrad years women are much nicer to each other. Older they get, the meaner they become to other women and maybe nicer to men. Of course it is not a rule but that is a trend I see."
